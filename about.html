About Us

Welcome to the Sierra Leone Football Agency (SLFA)

History

The SLFA was founded to manage and promote football activities in Sierra Leone. Over the years, it has developed local talent and strengthened national competitions.

Vision

To become one of the top football governing bodies in West Africa, providing equal opportunities and world-class facilities for all players.

Mission

To develop, organize, and promote football across Sierra Leone with integrity, professionalism, and transparency.
